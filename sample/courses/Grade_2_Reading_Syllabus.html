Grade 2 Reading Course

Course Overview

This dedicated Grade 2 Reading course is designed to strengthen emerging reading skills and foster a deeper love for books. Students will journey through a delightful collection of early chapter books and series, focusing on improving reading fluency, expanding vocabulary, and developing more advanced comprehension strategies. The course emphasizes understanding character development, plot progression, and making connections with the texts, all within an engaging and supportive learning environment.

Educational Approach

Advanced Phonics & Decoding Multi-syllable Words

Building on phonics knowledge to decode more complex words, including multi-syllable words and common word patterns, to enhance reading accuracy.

Building Reading Fluency & Expression

Activities designed to improve reading speed, accuracy, and prosody through guided oral reading, partner reading, and repeated exposure to texts.

Deeper Comprehension Strategies

Focus on developing comprehension skills such as identifying character traits and motivations, understanding plot development (problem/solution), recognizing setting details, and making simple inferences and predictions.

Curriculum Structure

The 30-session Grade 2 Reading course provides focused instruction using a variety of engaging early chapter books and popular series, typically spending 2-3 weeks on each selected title or set of stories. Each session will incorporate activities to reinforce phonics, build fluency, expand vocabulary in context, and develop key comprehension strategies. Students will participate in interactive discussions, learn to ask and answer questions about the text, and begin to make connections between different stories and their own experiences, fostering a path towards becoming lifelong readers.
