Level 6-7 Fundamentals of Writing

Course Overview

This foundational course for students in grades 6-7 is designed to build essential writing skills and confidence. The curriculum focuses on mastering core grammar, sentence structure, paragraph development, and basic essay organization. Students will also be introduced to creative writing techniques and the fundamentals of constructing simple analyses and arguments. The course emphasizes clarity, coherence, and correctness in writing, supported by LEX Intermediate vocabulary building and interactive exercises.

Key Writing Units & Concepts

Unit 1: Writing Fundamentals

Understanding basic sentence construction (simple, compound), identifying subjects and predicates, proper capitalization and punctuation. Developing topic sentences and supporting details.

Unit 2: Grammar, Structure & Vocabulary

Mastering parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs), subject-verb agreement, correct tense usage. Introduction to the 3/5/5 paragraph structure and basic essay form (introduction, body, conclusion). LEX Intermediate Vocabulary A-E.

Unit 3: The Editorial Process (Introduction)

Learning basic self-correction techniques for common errors in spelling, grammar, and punctuation. Understanding how to respond to simple feedback and identify areas for improvement. LEX Intermediate Vocabulary F-J.

Unit 4: Creative & Descriptive Writing

Using vivid adjectives and adverbs, incorporating sensory details to make writing more engaging. Writing simple narratives, descriptive paragraphs, and responding to creative prompts. LEX Intermediate Vocabulary K-O.

Unit 5: Analysis & Argument (Introduction)

Identifying the main idea in a text, finding supporting evidence for simple claims, and understanding the basics of persuasive writing (e.g., stating an opinion clearly). LEX Intermediate Vocabulary P-Z.

Writing Practice

Regular short assignments including paragraph writing, simple story creation, descriptive pieces, and basic responses to texts to reinforce learned concepts.

Educational Approach

Targeted Grammar & Mechanics Instruction

Clear and systematic instruction on fundamental grammar rules, punctuation, and sentence structure, reinforced through targeted exercises and writing practice.

Step-by-Step Writing Process

Guidance through the writing process, from brainstorming and outlining to drafting and simple revisions, to build confidence and good writing habits.

LEX Intermediate Vocabulary Building

Consistent vocabulary development using the LEX Intermediate curriculum (Letters A-Z), with weekly quizzes and activities to expand word knowledge and usage in writing.

Curriculum Structure

The 30-session course is organized into five key units, each spanning approximately six weeks. Units cover Writing Fundamentals; Grammar, Structure & Vocabulary; The Editorial Process; Creative & Descriptive Writing; and an Introduction to Analysis & Argument. Each unit involves direct instruction, interactive exercises, grammar drills, paragraph and short essay writing practice, and creative prompts. LEX Intermediate vocabulary is integrated weekly. The course aims to equip students with the core competencies needed for clear and effective written communication, preparing them for more advanced writing tasks.
